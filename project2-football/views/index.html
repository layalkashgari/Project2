Who We Are

The Future Set down beside a highway With all eyes on the Saudi Arabia’s 2030 Vision which states, “All success stories start with a vision, and successful visions are based on strong pillars’’ said by Crown Prince Mohammed bin Salman bin Abdulaziz, and in terms to achieve one of our visions Al Mamlaka Football Club have been established on the beginning of 2018 for talented player, which is represented by a united team of Saudi girls brought together for the love of competition, a passion for challenge, determination and commitment to represent the name of the Kingdom with the best possible way in all local and international tournaments

What We Do

Al Mamlaka Club was created to provide the opportunity for females' to play football, develop ,improve their skills, and to participate in training and competitive tournaments or matches in all around Khobar, Dammam, Riyadh and GCC with a very high and professional standers
